Trade-offs & Analysis

Single-Page Portal Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Immersive ambient audio/visual continuity (no page reloads)
  • Smooth glitch transition effects between sections
  • Feels like a premium, modern gaming portal

Cons

  • Single large file for layout (requires structured JS component rendering)
Multi-Page & Tarot Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Modular, easy to expand with more pages
  • Tarot style is highly thematic and visually neat

Cons

  • Multi-page breaks glitch animations during page loads
  • Tarot layout is less narrative-focused
